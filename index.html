Yongjiang Xu

Postdoctoral Researcher in High-Pressure Mineral Physics
Center for High Pressure Science and Technology Advanced Research (HPSTAR), Beijing

Research Interests

  • High-pressure solid–solid and solid–liquid phase transitions
  • Pressure calibration of large-volume press (LVP) systems
  • Clapeyron phase boundaries and thermodynamics of phase transitions
  • Experimental petrology and mineral physics under extreme conditions

Current Research

  • Systematic pressure calibration of multi-anvil and large-volume press systems
  • Compilation and re-evaluation of phase transition datasets for pressure markers
  • Determination of Clapeyron-type phase boundaries using in situ high P–T techniques
  • Integration of experimental data with thermodynamic and mechanical models
